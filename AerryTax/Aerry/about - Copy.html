About the Business

Aerry Tax & Accounting Pty Ltd is a Chartered Accounting practice. The practice is managed by experienced and qualified professionals, being CA / CPA member(s). Aerry Tax & Accounting Pty Ltd is a Registered Tax Agent, Registered Company Agent and Accountants.

Tarun Aerry, principle and founder has extensive experience of over 15 years in accounting industry. Tarun started his journey as a practicing chartered accountant in 1997 as a member of The institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and founded a partnership accounting firm few years later in 2001 with his school time mate & long-time associate under the name of “Anand Tarun & Co - Chartered Accountants” in India. Before starting the accounting practice in 2009 in Victoria, Tarun worked as “Financial Controller” in a not for profit organisation for over 5 years in Victoria. During these five years he also studies towards his CA membership of The Institute of Chartered Accountants in Australia and become full member of ICAA in 2010.

From a very moderate beginning from home in 2010, the business has grown substantially and is now providing a range of professional services to over 1000 clients that include individuals and businesses. The business office is very conveniently located in a commercial complex in Braybrook and about 10 minutes’ drive from Melbourne CBD with ample parking facility.

Aerry Tax & Accounting have been providing full range of accounting, taxation, business advisory and management reporting services to all business that include real estate agents, builders and property developers, restaurants, retail and / or wholesale traders, not for profit entities etc. We aim to help our clients identify and manage tax risk, maximise opportunities, minimise your tax exposure, take realistic tax positions and comply with all ATO requirements. We aim to assist Business Owners to move from where they are now to where they really want to be. The practice has advised and helped nurture a large number of business entities into becoming more profitable and successful businesses.

Our business philosophy is to allow business owners to not only save money over in-house accounting and ensure their compliance with tax laws, but also to make valuable management decisions from the numbers. We want you to realize your dream of building a successful business.
